55,000 Green Card Lotteries Played Every Year

The Green Card Lottery, officially known as the Diversity Visa Program, opens the door to the United States for 55,000 people every year, granting the chance to live, work, and direct path to U.S. Citizenship.

The Lottery Program Is Ending Forever

U.S. Senator Kennedy proposed the "Visa Lottery Repeal Act" (S. 859) to end the Diversity Visa Program. With political tides potentially shifting, the time to act is now more critical than ever.

This might be your last chance

Since the 1990s, the DV Lottery has granted hundreds of thousands of people Green Cards worldwide. The program offers a new beginning in the United States, but your chance to apply quickly disappears.
